Pi is the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter:
C = 2 * Pi * r = Pi * d
Pi = C / d
Pi Day is celebrated annually on 3/14 around the world.
People celebrate by eating pie, seeing how many digits of
Pi they can memorize, and learning more about Pi and why
it's so important.
For math lovers, Pi Day is one of the best days of the year.
